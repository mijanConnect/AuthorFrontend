About Me

Dr Denis

Dr. Dennis L. Ross, M.D., is a distinguished nephrologist recognized as one of America’s Most Influential Nephrologists by the International Association of Who’s Who. With a B.S. Cum Laude and an M.D. from the University of Nebraska, Dr. Ross serves as the President of Kansas Nephrology Physicians, P.A., and is a Clinical Professor at the University of Kansas School of Medicine. He holds certifications in Internal Medicine and Nephrology, with active medical licenses in Kansas, Nebraska, and Missouri.

Dr. Ross is also an accomplished author. His book, "The Perfect Match," was awarded 1st place in the 2022 Pencraft Awards for Literary Excellence, highlighting his talent not only in medicine but also in literature. Beyond his professional and literary achievements, he enjoys playing music, ballroom dancing, and spending cherished moments with his grandchildren.
